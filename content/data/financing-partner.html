ADB’s top 3 financing partners each committed over $300 million. Collectively, they account for 73% of sovereign cofinancing for 2019.

The biggest loan cofinancing for 2019 was with the Japan International Cooperation Agency for the Malolos-Clark Railway Project (hyperlink to PDS) in the Philippines, up to $2.1 billion of the project’s $2.75 billion budget.
